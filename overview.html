Building a Work of Art from Wood

By Mark on January 31, 2018

I knew the senior project was not about the actual result, it's about the learning and experience in the journey.

About

Mark Theis completed this project as a senior at Priory School in Portola Valley. His interests include theater, tennis, drone club, woodworking, music, and robotics. This website documents how he culminated those interests by crafting a musical instrument as a final high school project.

I hope you enjoy.
